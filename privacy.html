Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: email address and password when you create an account. We also collect usage data such as campaigns and influencer records you create within the app.

2. Payment Information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CreatorFlow service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send important service-related emails (account confirmation, billing)
  • Improve the product

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). Data is stored in the United States. We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the service (Firebase, Stripe) or as required by law.

6. Cookies

We use Firebase Authentication which stores a session token in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To do so, contact us at the email below.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at: support@creatorflow.io
