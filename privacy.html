Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

CreatorFlow ("we", "us", "our") operates the CreatorFlow web application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use CreatorFlow:

  • Account information: email address and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Usage data: campaigns, influencer contacts, and notes you create within the app
  • Payment information: billing details processed securely by Stripe — we never store card numbers

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send service-related emails (billing receipts, password resets)
  • To improve the application based on usage patterns

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), located in europe-west3. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the service (Firebase, Stripe).

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

Cookies

We use localStorage to maintain your session and preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@creatorflow.io
